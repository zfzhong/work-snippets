Reading Notes: things that surprises me
------------------------------------------
1. Parenthetic expression between commas:
   * Dates
   * Name, title in direct address
   * abbr etc., i.e., e.g.
   * no comma to separate a restrictive terms of identification
   * no comma to separate a restrictive clause

2. How to use semicolon ';' and comma ','.
   I had never been in the place before; besides, it was dark as a tomb.
   Man proposes, God disposes.

   The ';' indicates close relationship between to sentences. For short sentences or conversational sentences, ',' is used instead of ';'.
